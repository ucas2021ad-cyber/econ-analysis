## 📁 Project

### ⚡ Nunavut Energy System Analysis & Sankey Validation

* Analyzed Nunavut’s energy system, highlighting extreme reliance on imported diesel and infrastructure constraints
* Validated energy flow data using Sankey diagrams and compared results with official sources (CER)
* Identified discrepancies in sector-level energy use and explained differences due to modeling assumptions and data limitations
* Developed energy and carbon flow visualizations to illustrate system-level dependency and policy implications
* Evaluated economic vulnerability and provided policy recommendations for improving energy resilience
